About Me

I’m Sarah Luna, a self professed design nerd with serious HTML/CSS ninja skills. I specialize in Art Direction, Graphic Design, Illustration and WordPress Web Design. My passion is to craft and cultivate ideas that lead to beautiful, functional, one of a kind design.

I enjoy partnering with entrepreneurs and businesses to create a visual language that tells their story. My creative process builds upon research and analysis as the foundation for crafting compelling design solutions that help them connect with their audiences, meet their goals, and elevate their brands.

From fun personal projects to serious corporate gigs, I find that my design style tends to incorporate a love of color and illustration. I adhere to a high visual aesthetic standard, appreciate slick layout design, and find inspiration from the world around me. That includes the creative imagination of fashion and personal style, the eclectic beauty of home decor, and, last but not least, my beloved Los Angeles. Where the lifestyle and landscape is a treasure trove of urban eye candy.
